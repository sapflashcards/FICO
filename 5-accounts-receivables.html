Financial Terms Made Simple

Reconciliation Account

The bridge account that connects detailed customer records to the overall financial summary. Like a category label that groups all customer debts together.

Payment Terms

The agreement on when payment is due (e.g., "Net 30" means pay within 30 days). Like saying "you can pay me back next month."

Dunning Terms

Rules for reminding customers about overdue payments. Like setting up automatic reminders when someone hasn't paid you back yet.

Open Item

An unpaid invoice that's still waiting to be settled. Like an unchecked item on your to-do list that says "collect money from Tommy."
